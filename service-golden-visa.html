What is the UAE Golden Visa?

The UAE Golden Visa is a long-term residence visa that allows foreign nationals to live, work, and study in the UAE without the need for a national sponsor. This prestigious visa offers residency for 5 or 10 years, with automatic renewal, providing stability and security for you and your family.

  • Long-term residency (5-10 years)
  • Include family members
  • No sponsor required
  • Automatic renewal

Requirements & Documents

To apply for the UAE Golden Visa, you will need the following documents and information:

  • Valid passport with minimum 6 months validity
  • Recent passport-size photographs
  • Completed application form
  • Proof of eligibility for chosen category
  • Financial statements and bank records
  • Business documents (if applicable)
  • Contact details and address information
  • Email address for official correspondence
