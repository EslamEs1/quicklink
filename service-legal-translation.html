What is Legal Translation?

Legal translation is the precise conversion of legal documents from one language to another while maintaining their legal meaning, terminology, and context. Unlike general translation, legal translation requires specialized knowledge of legal systems, terminology, and concepts to ensure the translated document holds the same legal weight and meaning as the original.

Required Documents

To request legal translation services, you will need the following documents:

  • Original document to be translated (or a clear copy)
  • Valid Emirates ID or passport of the document owner
  • Any supporting documents that provide context
  • Previous translations of related documents (if available)
  • Specific requirements from the requesting authority
  • Contact details and address information
  • Phone number for communication
  • Email address for official correspondence
