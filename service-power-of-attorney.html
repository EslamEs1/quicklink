What is a Power of Attorney?

A Power of Attorney (POA) is a legal document that authorizes another person to act on your behalf in private affairs, business, or legal matters. The person granting the authority is known as the "Principal," while the person receiving the authority is the "Agent" or "Attorney-in-fact."

  • Legal authorization document
  • Delegate authority to others
  • Legally binding and valid
  • Customizable scope and duration

Requirements & Documents

To apply for a Power of Attorney, you will need the following documents and information:

  • Valid Emirates ID or passport of both parties
  • Completed application form (provided by us)
  • Specific details about powers being granted
  • Current address and contact details
  • Supporting documents for specific POA type
  • Trade license (for company POA)
  • Contact details and address information
  • Email address for official correspondence
