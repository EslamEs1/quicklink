What is Signature Notarization?

Signature notarization is the process of having a certified notary public witness and authenticate your signature on legal documents. This process ensures that the signature is genuine, the signer is properly identified, and the document is legally binding under UAE law.

  • Legal document authentication
  • UAE law compliant
  • Proper identification verification
  • Legally binding certification

Requirements for Signature Notarization

To ensure a smooth notarization process, please bring the following documents and information.

  • Valid ID Documents: Emirates ID, passport, or driving license
  • Original Documents: All documents to be notarized in original form
  • Complete Information: All required fields must be filled before notarization
  • Physical Presence: All signatories must be present for notarization
