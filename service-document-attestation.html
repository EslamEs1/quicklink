Requirements & Documents

To attest your documents, you will need the following documents and information:

  • Original documents to be attested
  • Valid passport copy
  • UAE residence visa or entry stamp
  • Current address and contact details
  • Supporting documents (if required)
  • Business documents (for commercial attestation)
  • Contact details for official correspondence
  • Email address for document delivery
