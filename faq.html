Power of Attorney Services

We offer various types of Power of Attorney including General POA, Special POA, Real Estate POA, Vehicle POA, Bank Account POA, Business Management POA, and more. Each type serves specific purposes and requirements.

Required documents include valid passport, Emirates ID, recent photographs, and specific documents related to the type of POA (e.g., property documents for real estate POA, vehicle registration for vehicle POA).

Company Establishment

We can help establish various types of companies including LLC, Free Zone companies, Branch offices, Representative offices, and other business structures in the UAE. We provide guidance for both mainland and free zone establishments.

Company establishment typically takes 5-15 business days depending on the type of company and jurisdiction. Free zone companies are usually faster to establish than mainland companies.

Golden Visa Services

Golden Visa is available for investors, entrepreneurs, specialized talents, outstanding students, and executives. Requirements vary by category, including minimum investment amounts, qualifications, and experience levels.

Yes, Golden Visa holders can include their spouse and children under 18 years of age. The visa provides long-term residency for the entire family without the need for annual renewals.
